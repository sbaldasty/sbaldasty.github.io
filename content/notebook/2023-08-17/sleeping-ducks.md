---
title: Sleeping ducks
topics: [trivia]
---

I remembered hearing on a podcast long ago that ducks can sleep with one eye open, letting half their brain sleep at a time. It seemed implausible when I repeated it but Kirsten Kouwenhoven found an article that [corroborates](https://www.gi.alaska.edu/alaska-science-forum/bird-brains-stay-alert-while-ducks-sleep) that memory.
