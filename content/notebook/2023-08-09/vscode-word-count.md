I needed to track the number of words I had in a submission. I found a [Microsoft plugin](https://github.com/Microsoft/vscode-wordcount) that shows the number of words a document at the bottom of the window. However since being able to select text and quickly see the word count of the selection would also be helpful, I continued searching and found [another plugin](https://github.com/cjwit/selection-word-count) to help with that.
