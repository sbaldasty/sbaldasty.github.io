With the termination of Google Album Archive, I was really hoping to store my website images on Google Photos and be able to pull them from there to process. One new thought I had was query from the API on the [description field](https://support.google.com/photos/thread/164570/can-i-keep-my-descriptions-associated-w-my-photos-when-i-download-them-to-my-computer). I ultimately decided to maintain an [album](https://photos.google.com/share/AF1QipN9Vhb3lEHUSetk4Ev5krB_CKtPX0Gl_ntQN6V_2gaQOnnUJylVJ26l60Q6mq7KfA) and pull the files from it manually as needed. My scripts act on the filenames used by Google Photos; at least for now it is [too cumbersome](https://support.google.com/photos/thread/1261014?hl=en&msgid=1341082) to rename them.
