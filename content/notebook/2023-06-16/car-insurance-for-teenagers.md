---
title: Car insurance for teenagers
---

Research into the process of setting my daughter up with a car suggested I should expect [insurance premiums](https://quotewizard.com/auto-insurance/cost-of-teen-car-insurance) of up to 6000 USD per year, done legally and ethically (the practice of listing a teenager as an additional driver on the policy when they are in fact the primary driver is a form of car insurance fraud called [fronting](https://www.confused.com/car-insurance/guides/what-is-fronting)). It turned out with a liability-only policy, an old car, having had her listed on my policy as an additional driver for two years, and other discounts, that she could be insured for _much_ less.
