---
title: Vermont vehicle registration
---

Buyers must [register their vehicle](https://dmv.vermont.gov/sites/dmv/files/documents/VD-119-Vehicle_Registration_Tax_Title.pdf) after a private sale. The vehicle may also have to pass emissions test, which _appears_ to be different from an inspection - or not; there are several [testing locations](https://www.emissions.org/loc/vermont-emissions-testing/chittenden-county-vt/), but our vehicle may be [exempt because of its age](https://dmv.vermont.gov/enforcement-and-safety/vehicle-inspections). The DMV has a [wizard](https://appengine.egov.com/apps/vt/DMV/requireddocumentswizard) to help identify the requisite documents and fees. We need a **Registration Application**, **Odometer Discolsure Statement**, and **Bill of Sale** according to the wizard. [Temporary registration and plates](https://mydmvtempplate.vermont.gov/) can bridge the gap between the application and when the official registration and plates arrive.
