I explored using [taxonomies](https://gohugo.io/content-management/taxonomies/) to reuse content in daily notebook pages and topical pages. Jessica Smith's blog shows a [nice example](https://www.jayeless.net/2021/04/taxonomies-archive-hugo.html). Ultimately the way Hugo manages taxonomy and term pages was too [inflexible](https://discourse.gohugo.io/t/adding-content-for-taxonomies/6343).
