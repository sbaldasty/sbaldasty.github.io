Both pandas and scikit-learn can categorize and [one hot encode](https://stackoverflow.com/questions/37292872/how-can-i-one-hot-encode-in-python) numerical data. Interestingly, Tom de Ruijter documents an [approach](https://medium.com/bigdatarepublic/integrating-pandas-and-scikit-learn-with-pipelines-f70eb6183696) for integrating pandas data transformations into scikit-learn. I stuck with pandas because it was adequate for my needs.
