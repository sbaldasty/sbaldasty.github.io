I checked out the _Introduction to Programming_ classroom to familiarize myself with the logistics and equipment there. I will probably want to use my personal laptop to share examples during lectures so I tried connecting it to the projector, but I didn't find a way. I found UVM maintains [good documentation](https://www.uvm.edu/it/classrooms/room/votey-hall-209/) about the classroom though. The way to present on my own laptop is to log into the presenter computer which is connected to the projector, start a Microsoft Teams meeting, join the meeting from my personal laptop, and finally share my screen.
