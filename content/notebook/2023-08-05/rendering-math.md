title: Rendering math
---

I found numerous discussions about the lack of native server-side LaTeX / MathJax rendering on the Hugo forums. It appears to be possible though with the use of an [external service](https://discourse.gohugo.io/t/rendering-latex-as-a-compile-step/42145/3). I'd rather not depend on another service for my build, but it works locally and in my GitHub pipeline so I am including it for now.
