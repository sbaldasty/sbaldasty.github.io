## Google Photos links

I use python scripts to download and process my Google Photos images for display on my website. Google does not provide permanent direct links through Photos though, so I rely on a [technique](https://stackoverflow.com/questions/39508631/how-to-get-direct-permanent-link-to-public-image-on-google-photos) involving Google Album Archive. However, next month Archive goes away. This [_digital inspiration_ service](https://www.labnol.org/embed/google/photos/) may be an alternative but I haven't researched it yet.

## Vermont vehicle registration

Buyers must [register their vehicle](https://dmv.vermont.gov/sites/dmv/files/documents/VD-119-Vehicle_Registration_Tax_Title.pdf) after a private sale. The vehicle may also have to pass emissions test, which _appears_ to be different from an inspection; there are several [testing locations](https://www.emissions.org/loc/vermont-emissions-testing/chittenden-county-vt/), but our vehicle may be [exempt because of its age](https://dmv.vermont.gov/enforcement-and-safety/vehicle-inspections). The DMV has a [wizard](https://appengine.egov.com/apps/vt/DMV/requireddocumentswizard) to help identify the requisite documents and fees. We need a **Registration Application**, **Odometer Discolsure Statement**, and **Bill of Sale** according to the wizard.
