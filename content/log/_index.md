---
title: Daily Notes
---

As an experiment, I'm publishing the notes I take throughout the day on my website. They may be poorly written, incomplete, factually inaccurate, or lack enough context to make sense. They may also be useful to someone. I am curious to learn what information I am comfortable sharing publicly versus what information I am not, whether knowing my notes are publicly available will encourage me to make them higher quality, whether writing more will improve my own expertise and attention to detail, and whether the act of sharing them will increase my sense of interest and engagement in the things I do with my life.
