Vuvuzela: Scalable private messaging resistant to traffic analysis
2025-02-11
As applied conventionally, differential privacy facilitates a compromise between the utility of a dataset and the privacy of the parties who appear in it; but as applied in the secure messaging system Vuvuzela, differential privacy facilitates a compromise between the privacy of the clients and the economy of the system in terms of compute, network traffic, message latency, message throughput, and so forth.
