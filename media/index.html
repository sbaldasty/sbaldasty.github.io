Introduction to choreographies
2025-02-22
Choreographies describe interactions between processes in distributed systems. Inference rules model the many possible traces that arise from concurrency in the system. Specialized programming languages promote compliance and conformance in processes. They help avoid process starvation. Some languages even produce independent executable build artifacts per process.
Vuvuzela: Scalable private messaging resistant to traffic analysis
2025-02-11
Differential privacy usually balances the utility of a dataset with the privacy of the individuals in it. In the secure messaging system Vuvuzela, differential privacy balances the privacy of the users with the efficiency of the system in terms of compute, latency, and network traffic. Privacy parameters translate nicely into the percent confidence a powerful adversary can gain over many rounds that Alice and Bob ever communicated.
