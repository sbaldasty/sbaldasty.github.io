Exploring the claim...

During a penumbral lunar eclipse, when the earth is almost directly between the sun and moon, the shadow of the earth on the moon is consistently round.
Claim usages

This claim is used to support:

The earth is shaped basically like a sphere.

Personal evaluation

I have seen pictures of lunar eclipses showing a circular shadow, and twice I even observed it in person! Admittedly though, without presupposing the modern model of the solar system, assuming the phenomenon observed during an eclipse is the earth's shadow is a bit of a leap.

I think this claim is True.
