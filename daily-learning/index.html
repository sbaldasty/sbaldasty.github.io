Sleeping ducks

2023-08-17

I heard on a podcast long ago that ducks can sleep with one eye open, maintaining a lessened alertness while letting half their brain sleep at a time. It seemed implausible when I repeated it, but my friend found an article that corroborates my memory. Also, ducks like to swim in the warm circular pools at a local water treatment facility.

Grayscale mode for smartphones

2023-08-02

Smartphone users can switch their phones to grayscale mode, which allegedly helps reduce addiction. My phone is an important tool in my life. I have found this to be helpful. Color is usually not necessary (but sometimes it is).

Vermont vehicle registration

2023-06-17

Buyers must register their vehicle after a private sale. The vehicle may also have to pass emissions test, which appears to be different from an inspection - or not; there are several testing locations, but our vehicle may be exempt because of its age. The DMV has a wizard to help identify the requisite documents and fees. We need a Registration Application, Odometer Discolsure Statement, and Bill of Sale according to the wizard. Temporary registration and plates can bridge the gap between the application and when the official registration and plates arrive.

Teen car insurance

2023-06-16

Research into the process of setting my daughter up with a car suggested I should expect insurance premiums of up to $6000 per year, done legally and ethically (the practice of listing a teenager as an additional driver on the policy when they are in fact the primary driver is a form of car insurance fraud called fronting). It turns out with a liability-only policy, an old car, having had her listed on my policy as an additional driver for two years, and other discounts, that she could be insured for much less.
