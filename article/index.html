Who writes anymore?

Writing feels uncomfortable in a time when artificial intelligence and other humans publish better writing everywhere. My experiences and ideas often feel ordinary to me. The things I learn feel commonplace. My chances of ever contributing something useful feel small. But there is no harm in trying, and sometimes good things happen when I ignore that judgemental defeatist voice.
